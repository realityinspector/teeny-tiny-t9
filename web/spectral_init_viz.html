Spectral Initialization via Pretrained Extraction

Transferring SVD structure from trained models to accelerate training from scratch
3.22x faster convergence on GPT-2 small (124M params)
Step 1 — Extract

SVD Decomposition

Decompose each weight matrix of a pretrained GPT-2 via SVD. Group by type (attention, FFN, embedding). Compress each group's average singular value spectrum to 8 DCT coefficients.
Step 2 — Shape + Align

Spectral Initialization

For each fresh random weight: SVD it, replace singular values with the extracted spectral shape, blend singular vectors 50% toward pretrained directions. Reconstruct.
Step 3 — Train

Accelerated Training

Train with 2x learning rate (spectral init tolerates it; orthogonal diverges), spike-skip for gradient stability, grad clip 0.5. Converges 3.22x faster.
Winit = Ufresh · diag(Sextracted) · blend(Vfresh, Vpretrained, 0.5)T
