The Key Insight

Spectral Comparison

Random initialization produces nearly flat singular value spectra. Pretrained weights show steep, structured decay. Spectral initialization reshapes the spectrum to match — encoding task structure into the initialization without copying any learned features.

The Math

SVD Decomposition of Pretrained Weights

Every weight matrix W decomposes as U · Σ · VT. The singular vectors encode learned directional structure; the singular values encode the energy distribution across those directions.

Pretrained GPT-2 attention weight (768 × 2304) — SVD reveals structured singular vectors and steep spectral decay

Pretrained Spectral Fingerprints

Each weight group in GPT-2 has a distinctive spectral shape. We extract these via DCT compression (32 coefficients) and use them to initialize fresh weights.
